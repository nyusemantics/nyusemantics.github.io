NYU Semantics Group

We are an informal talk series focusing on semantics and related subfields. Meetings take place at 10:15am on Fridays, in room #103 of 10 Washington Place, unless noted otherwise. Talks are 60 minutes in length, followed by a 30 minute Q&A session.

To contact the organizers or to subscribe to the NYU semantics mailing list, please email semantics-group[at]nyu.edu.
